AI Sleeping Agent Automatic Support

What the AI does automatically

  • detects when a child is stuck
  • offers hints and easier wording
  • switches to read-aloud mode
  • suggests the next best mission
  • saves progress and topic interests
  • creates teacher and parent summaries
This keeps routine learning support active so children can stay curious and continue exploring.

Safety and support limits

  • no hidden recording
  • no raw audio storage
  • no biometric voiceprints
  • no unsafe autonomous decisions
  • major safety, health, or account concerns still go to adults
The AI helper supports learning and curiosity, not adult responsibility for safety.
