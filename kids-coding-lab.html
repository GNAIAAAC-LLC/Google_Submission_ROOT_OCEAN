It supports school curriculum, extra practice, creativity, and different learning levels, while the AI Sleeping Agent helps when kids get stuck on normal learning steps.

AI Sleeping Agent Automatic Support

Automatic functions

  • detect repeated mistakes
  • offer hints automatically
  • open extra practice missions
  • switch to easier or harder mode
  • save progress and skill growth
  • prepare parent and teacher summaries
This helps children handle normal learning frustration with less routine interruption.

Important safety rule

  • no raw audio stored
  • no biometric voiceprints
  • no hidden recording
  • no unsafe autonomous decisions
  • serious safety, health, or account problems still go to adults
The AI helper supports learning only. It does not replace adult responsibility.
