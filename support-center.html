Safe Communication Rules

The support center should use safe and realistic communication language.

What to say

  • request received
  • support review in progress
  • follow-up depends on availability
  • voice or phone features where supported
  • contact details logged with permission

What not to say

  • guaranteed live support 24/7 unless true
  • fully automated issue resolution unless built
  • every phone call is recorded unless true
  • all services are always instantly available

Safe website note

Customer phone and voice communications may be logged in the support workflow to help manage requests, follow-up, and service coordination, subject to user permission, platform readiness, and applicable privacy rules.

Support limitation notice

Voice Hub can remain an optional tool, but the Contact page should remain the main customer support path. Live telephony, automatic call recording, and provider-based routing require a backend system and telephony provider before they can be honestly claimed as active.
