The SSGPT6 Kids Avatar Helper is designed to support children during learning with school-based curriculum guidance, extra practice, resource-based learning, and friendly AI help when they get stuck.

It supports different kinds of learners: beginners, struggling learners, average learners, advanced learners, readers, visual learners, and kids who need extra step-by-step support.

Important Rule

The avatar helper is designed to reduce routine interruptions during learning, but it should not replace adult support for anything involving emotional distress, health, safety, account issues, or major decisions.
